Wedding Reception Accomidations

Reception Set-Up Services

  • We provide set up and takedown service for your event for an additional cost. During our planning process, we will work with you to arrange all details prior to the event so you have one less worry on the day of your event.
  • Professional lighting can be a game changer for your reception. If your venue feels too dim, if you want to spotlight original architecture, or even if you just want to add some color to your reception, using our professional lighting service will make your venue shine.
  • Our venue can fit up to 500 people, and has parking aranged for events
  • Chairs and tables can be rented for a standard fee
  • DJ and needed equiptment availabe for rental but must be booked in advance
  • Outdoor venue available upon request, contact us for more information

Reception Food Service

  • Access to on site chef and team who can make a meal, or refreshments for your guests
  • Outside catering is permitted
  • We have a relationship with many food truck companies, for more information please contact us
  • Guests stand in line, but do not help themselves. They are served by chefs and/or servers from behind the buffet line.
  • Kitchen access for food preparation and serving, if needed for outside vendors or family set-up
  • Food tables and cutlery is provided for a stamdard fee
  • More services can be requested, and we would be happy to work out the details with you. We have a flexible team who is happy as long as you are, and will do all that they can to make your wedding day special
