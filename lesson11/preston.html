Why We Love Idaho

By: Will Jensen

Idaho, home of the brave. Home of the beauty. Home of the potato. We love our state, and have the most state pride out of every state. Yes, we know that is a bold statement, but we mean it. Here’s why. The number of activities that can be done here are endless. With 4 distinct seasons, you have an opportunity to participate in the seasonal activities that many other states don’t (cough, cough, Arizona). With a vast variety of landscapes, that also increases your options for activities. Not only do these things make out state more fun, but also the most beautiful, with astonishing changes from season to season. Show your Idaho pride, and tell your family what you appreciate about this great state.
