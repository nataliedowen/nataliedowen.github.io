Target Audience

The target audience for Fast Cast Weather is the residents of the 3 locations for which the site will report the forecast, as well as those traveling to those locations. The intended gender is both male and female, and the age range is 16-65 years old. Those under 16 will likely depend on their parents for weather information, and those above 65 generally rely on more traditional forms of weather forcast information. The will access the site from their smart phone, tablet, or computer.
