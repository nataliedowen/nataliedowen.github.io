Heading Font

The typeface that I have chosen for all heading on the site is Playfair Display. This typeface will also be used on the navagation bar. It is displayed as the heading above. It is available on Google Fonts.

Body Copy Font

To contrast the serif typeface used to display the headings, I have chosen a sans serif typeface for the body copy. I feel that thus contrast looks professional, and helps distinguish between the body copy and headings with ease. I have chosen the Roboto typeface, which is also available on Google Fonts. The text you are currently reading is displayed in the Roboto typeface.
